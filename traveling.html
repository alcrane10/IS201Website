Travel Postcards

A curated collection of cobblestones, gelato, and memories.

01. Cinque Terre, Italy

Visited: April 2025

My favorite part was walking all around the city! It ended up being a crazy hike but we were laughing and having so much fun as a family. Finished off the hike by stopping and getting some gelato. It was so delicious!

02. Zurich, Switzerland

Visited: May 2025

Hiking through the mountain range and countryside of Switzerland was incredible! The cute cows along the side of the road while enjoying some Swiss chocolate. An unforgettable experience!

03. Dugi Otok, Croatia

Visited: June 2025

I served my mission in Croatia and my last area was along the coast. While visiting it was such a special experience visiting all the members as well as exploring the islands. Just off the coast of Croatia lies the beautiful Dugi Otok!

04. Paris, France

Visited: June 2025

The Eiffel Tower was so iconic! It was my first time and I had no clue what to expect. My favorite part was staying late into the night and watching it sparkle!
